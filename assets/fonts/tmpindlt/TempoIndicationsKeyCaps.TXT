Tempo Indications Font: Key Caps^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^________________________________________________upper- and lowercase letters = bold face Times Roman													 	numbers 0 - 9 = plain face Times Roman________________________________________________Musical symbols:^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^			* = breve (double whole note)			& = semibreve (whole note)			^ = minim (half note)			% = crotchet (quarter note)			$ = quaver, curved beam  (eighth note)			# = semiquaver, curved beam  (sixteenth note)			@ = quaver, straight beam  (eighth note)			! = semiquaver, straight beam  (sixteenth note)________________________________________________			full stop = dot (for dotted notes)			_ (shift-hyphen)  = tie________________________________________________		 { = left-pointing arrow	   } = right-pointing arrow ________________________________________________(c) Matthew Hindson, 1996.
